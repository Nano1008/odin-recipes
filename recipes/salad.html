Salad

Description

A salad is a dish consisting of mixed ingredients, frequently vegetables. They are typically served chilled or at room temperature, though some can be served warm. Condiments and salad dressings, which exist in a variety of flavors, are used to make a salad.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Green bell pepper
  • Tomato
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place onion and bell pepper in a microwave-safe bowl; heat in microwave on high until soft, about 1 to 2 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  3. Combine onion, bell pepper, salad greens, deli meat, and tomato in a large salad bowl. Sprinkle with onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper; toss well to mix.
  4. Pour on salad dressing; toss well and serve.
